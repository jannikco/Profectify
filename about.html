Our story

Started as consultants. Grew into operators.

Profectify began as a senior HR and technology consultancy. Our founder spent more than a decade helping companies grow teams, digitalise processes and make better technical decisions — serving clients across tech, finance, communications and professional services.

Somewhere along the way, the advice got harder to give without getting our own hands dirty. So we stopped just writing decks and started building products. Today Profectify is a group of nine companies — operating platforms that serve families, teachers, school leaders, developers and print-shop owners every day.

We still take on select consulting engagements. But our centre of gravity has shifted — we build more than we advise now, and it shows.
